About Me

After completing my Bachelor's Degree in Music Business with a concentration in Jazz Trumpet at the Crane School of Music, I was offered the chance to study with Dizzy Gillespie's apprentice, Jon Faddis at Purchase College to pursue my Master of Music. After completing my education, I briefly worked in the music industry and realized that I did not want to be a part of the business side of music. I just wanted to play my instrument for fun! Shortly after this, I began working at Georgia State University as an Academic Advisor for students within the College of the Arts. After a few months, I began to CRAVE the chance to utilize my creative problem solving skills.

This is when I decided to quit my full-time job to attend DigitalCrafts, a coding bootcamp where I am currently studying JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, React, Redux, Node.js, Python, and other development technologies. While I still love to play my trumpet, I truly believe I have found my calling as a Software Engineer.

I am absolutely thrilled to say that I am now a Full Stack Developer! I can't wait to utilize my past experience in creative problem solving, improvisation and building personal relationships to excel on a team as a Software Engineer.
